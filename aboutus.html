About Us

History

Kitisuru Youth Soccer Academy(KYSA) is a registered football academy that was founded in 2022. It started with 18 players and has since grown to over 150 players. The players cut across the lower economic background hailing within the Mwimuto,Wangige and Kabete environs. The academy was formed as a football sports system to foster talent, teamwork and personal development among young atheletes in our community to enlighten them about social vices such as alchoholism, drug abuse and discipline.
